Patient Record Management System

The management of patient records is one of the most important processes in a healthcare institution. This is where the patient's information, especially the medical records and history is being collected. Maintaining the privacy, the protection, and the security of the patient's information is the healthcare institution's responsibility. Despite being in the era of modern technology, there are healthcare institutions that are still adapting the traditional system of management though the said system has limitations in terms of information accessibility, security, and real-time update.
