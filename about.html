Patient Record Management System

The project developers will develop the Patient Records Management System to streamline and automate the patient's information, treatment history in the healthcare, and the patient's medical records. The system will benefit the institution and the healthcare professionals for the system provide easier and faster access to the information they need. The system will also benefit the patients for the system also provides security to the information and medical records..
